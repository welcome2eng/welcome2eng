About me

I am an English teacher with 20 years of experience. I began working in the profession already in the second year of studies - then on courses for children aged 9-10. After graduation, I became a teacher in state schools - middle school and secondary school.. During 20 years of work, I have helped hundreds of graduates to succeed in the secondary school-leaving examination, and younger students to get to their dream secondary schools. Years of experience have taught me two things: firstly, there are no "unteachable" students, and secondly, partnership and a friendly atmosphere are just as important in the teaching process as the issues we learn, because they largely contribute to success in learning.

In addition to teaching, since 2000 I have also been dealing with text translations. For fourteen years I was cooperating with academics of the Częstochowa University of Technology, for whom I translated scientific articles in the field of finance, management, information systems and neural networks. At the same time, I also translated into Polish the book "Invest Like a Shark" by James Deporre ("Zostań rekinem giełdowym" ed. Difin). I have also done translations in life sciences for SDL Poland. I work in the Trados environment.
